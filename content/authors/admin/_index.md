## About Me
I am currently in my first year of the Ph.D. program at the [University of Central Florida](https://www.ece.ucf.edu/), under the supervision of [Prof. George Atia](https://www.eecs.ucf.edu/~atia/).

I earned my Master's degree in Computer Engineering from [New York University (NYU)](https://www.nyu.edu/), where I had the opportunity to work as a research assistant in the [AI4CE LAB](https://ai4ce.github.io/people/), focusing on visual room rearrangement with [Wenyu Han](https://wenyuhan-lina.github.io/) and mentored by [Prof. Chen Feng](https://engineering.nyu.edu/faculty/chen-feng). I also led the VIP self-drive team, working on the embodied AI platform and competing in a maze exploration challenge in both simulation and real-world environments. Prior to that, I conducted research in the [MMVC](https://nyuad.nyu.edu/en/research/faculty-labs-and-projects/multimedia-and-visual-computing-lab.html) lab under [Prof. Yi Fang](https://engineering.nyu.edu/faculty/yi-fang), exploring reinforcement learning for robotic grasping.

Before pursuing my Master's, I completed my Bachelor's degree with honors in Computer Science at the [University of Nottingham](https://www.nottingham.ac.uk/), where I researched multi-camera gaze estimation for long distances under the supervision of [Prof. Ying Weng](https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=GmG5E4UAAAAJ&hl=en).
