Hi, glad to see you here. I'm currently pursuing a Master of Science degree at New York University, with a concentration in Computer Engineering. Previously, I completed my undergraduate study at University of Nottingham with first-class honors in Computer Science.

Research Interests
======

Recognizing that the ultimate aim of all research is to apply it in real-world scenarios, enhancing the interaction between humans and machines is interesting and challenging. My areas of research interest are in computer vision, robotic perception, and machine learning algorithms. I aspire to transform these algorithms into practical engineering solutions that aid in human construction. Specifically, my research focuses on: (1) visual navigation, (2) visual mobile manipulation, and (3) Large Language Models
